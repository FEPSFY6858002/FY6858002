Conference paper: Medical Applications of Particle Accelerators

Abstract

Particle accelerators have expanded their repertoire to include medical applications by utilising the inventions of cyclotrons and linear accelerators (linacs). They are essential in the process of producing radionuclides for diagnostic imaging. The main example being PET scans. This will be explored and expanded upon in this paper about the medical application of particle accelerators.
